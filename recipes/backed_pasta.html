Baked Pasta

Intro

A staple in the house is a baked pasta similar to a baked Ziti. This is made multiple times a month and is absolutely loved by all. We have tried using various types of meats and have settled on grounk pork as the best option for the flavor it brings to the dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground sausage
  • 1 lb of penne pasta
  • 1 large can of tomato sauce (largest size available)
  • Mozerrella cheese grated
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Garlic (finely chopped)
  • additional spices as desired

Directions

  1. Cook the sausage and garlic
  2. Start the pot of water with some salt in it for the pasta
  3. Break the sausage down as cooking into small ground up bits
  4. Once cooked, add the tomato sauce, basil, salt, pepper, oregano, and any additional spices desired
  5. Simmer sauce while pasta cooks
  6. Cook pasta according to directions of pasta container
  7. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees
  8. Once pasta is ready, drain and mix together with pasta sauce
  9. In 9x13 pan, line bottom of tray with pasta mixture
  10. Layer on shredded cheese
  11. Repeat until all pasta is in pan and has a layer of cheese on top
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes covered
  13. Uncover for 5-10 minutes of baking at end
  14. Let cool for a few minutes and then enjoy
