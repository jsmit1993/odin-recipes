Pasta Bolognese

Intro

One of our staple recipes in our house a simple meat sauce and pasta. We styled it after a pasta bolognese. The ingredients and recipe are listed below

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of pasta (your choice of pasta)
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1 medium size can of tomato sauce (actual size)
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Brown the beef with the crushed/finely cut garlic
  2. While meat is cooking, start a pot of water to boil pasta
  3. Break down the beef into small bits as cooking
  4. Once fully cooked, add the can of tomato sauce, salt, pepper, basil, and oregano seasonings to taste preference
  5. Let the meat sauce simmer while water starts to boil
  6. Follow directions on pasta container for cooking time - this time will be used to allow sauce to simmer for a longer time
  7. Drain pasta once complete and ready, remove both sauce and pasta from heat and mix together in a single pot
